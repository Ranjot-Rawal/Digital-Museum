Famous Paintings

Mona Lisa (1503-1519)

Painted by Leonardo da Vinci, the Mona Lisa is one of the most famous artworks in the world. It is admired for its realistic detail, innovative techniques, and the subject's enigmatic smile. The painting is currently displayed in the Louvre Museum in Paris.

The Starry Night (1889)

Created by Vincent van Gogh, The Starry Night depicts a dramatic night sky filled with swirling stars above a quiet village. It is regarded as one of the greatest masterpieces of Post-Impressionist art.

The Last Supper (1495–1498)

The Last Supper is a mural by Leonardo da Vinci that portrays the moment Jesus announces that one of his disciples will betray him. It is one of the most studied and reproduced artworks in history.

Girl with a Pearl Earring (1665)

Painted by Johannes Vermeer, this masterpiece is renowned for its simplicity, lighting, and the captivating gaze of the subject. It remains one of the most recognized paintings in the world.
